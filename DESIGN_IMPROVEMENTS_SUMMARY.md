## 🎨 Design System

### Colors
- **Primary**: Blue (#2563EB) to Indigo (#4F46E5)
- **Success**: Green (#10B981)
- **Warning**: Amber (#F59E0B) to Orange (#F97316)
- **Danger**: Red (#DC2626) to Rose (#F43F5F)
- **Mental Health**: Pink (#EC4899) to Rose (#F43F5F)

### Typography
- **Headers**: Bold, 2xl-4xl sizes
- **Body**: Medium weight, sm-base sizes
- **Labels**: Uppercase, xs size with tracking

### Spacing
- **Cards**: p-6 (24px padding)
- **Gaps**: gap-4 to gap-6 (16-24px)
- **Margins**: mb-4 to mb-8 (16-32px)

### Shadows
- **Cards**: shadow-lg, shadow-xl on hover
- **Buttons**: shadow-md
- **Overlays**: shadow-2xl

### Borders
- **Cards**: border-2 (2px solid)
- **Badges**: border-2 with rounded-full
- **Inputs**: border-2 with focus rings

### Transitions
- **Duration**: 200ms for most interactions
- **Easing**: Default ease for smooth animations

## 📱 Responsive Design

### Breakpoints
- **Mobile**: Single column layouts

- **Stacked navigation** on small screens
